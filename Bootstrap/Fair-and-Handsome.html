Hello.!

In 2005, Fair And Handsome launched the first-ever cream specially formulated for men. With this breakthrough innovation, it flagged off the Men’s Grooming revolution in India, taking it beyond shaving. This move was the culmination of years of meticulous research and development in understanding how men’s skin is significantly different from women’s skin. Healthy and radiant skin are no longer exclusive for women. The launch of Fair And Handsome provided men with the opportunity to break free from being closet users of women’s skin care products and still ace Handsome-giri.
